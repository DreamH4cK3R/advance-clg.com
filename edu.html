Welcome to Advanced Institute of Education (AIE)


Advanced Institute of Education (AIE), a constituent of Advanced Educational Institutions occupies a unique position in academic circles. The institute is situated on the outskirts of Palwal in a sprawling 25 acres campus with excellent infrastructure in science, computer, educational technology, and language laboratories, a well-equipped library and hostels for both boys and girls. It epitomizes the extension of the knowledge tradition embedded in the rich intellectual and cultural heritage of India. AIE has been envisioned to introduce and initiate future teachers through the process of transformation into evolved beings who can lead and guide the children of tomorrow towards the knowledge century in a true sense. With holistic training components, value education and skill-orientation as the mainstay of the teacher education, AIE has a vision of empowering the nation and the world with teachers who can take the onus of shaping the next generation of students with a sense of responsibility for the nation and an orientation for internationalism. Teaching at AIE emphasizes grooming students for surviving in this globally competitive world and become world class teachers. To achieve the goal of providing quality education, various innovative practices are used as transactional strategies. Students are provided with real life experiences. Special emphasis is laid on school experience program. Under the supervision of expert faculty, our students are provided with rich school experience where they can polish their teaching competencies. The institute has taken up the process of modernization through the infusion of ICT and promotion of research in the field of education.
