ADVANCED INSTITUTE OF PHARMACY(AIP)


Advanced Institute of Pharmacy (AIP) was established in 2007 with a view to provide job oriented professional courses B. Pharmacy, M. Pharmacy (Regulatory affairs) and D. Pharmacy. Bachelor of Pharmacy and Master of Pharmacy courses are affiliated to Pt. B.D Sharma University of Health Sciences and approved by Pharmacy council of India, New Delhi, AICTE and state government and Diploma of Pharmacy is affiliated to Haryana State Board of Technical Education, Panchkula. We not only provide quality education in Pharmaceutical sciences but also an ideal platform for the overall development of the students. Laboratories of AIP are well equipped with instruments and machines. The students are specially trained in practical knowledge to cope up with industrial problems. Teaching methods at AIP emphasize on direct personal contact between the teaching faculty and the students. Our students excel in the university exams and are placed in the university merit list every year. All the AIP students can get good jobs in reputed companies through campus interviews and direct recruitment.
